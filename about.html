About L.A. Rights

We provide affordable legal services because we believe that everybody deserves the right to an attorney.

At L.A. Rights Law Office, our clients are treated with respect and dignity. We will evaluate your situation, and offer sound, trustworthy advice. Our firm offers a variety of immigration services including family petitions, naturalization and other nonimmigrant visas. The information contained on our website is general information concerning our immigration laws.

About Deniece Kuwahara

Attorney at Law

University of Colorado at Boulder

Deniece was born and raised in Los Angeles. She brings years of experience in immigration law and is a strong and passionate believer in immigrant rights. She brings passion to her work and has devoted her practice exclusively to assisting immigrants with the complex legal system that surrounds immigration matters.
